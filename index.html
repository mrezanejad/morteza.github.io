About Me

I am a Senior Machine Learning Scientist at Recursion. I am also an adjunct scientist at the University of Toronto (courtesy appointment). Previously, I was an Arts & Science Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Toronto working under the supervision of Professor Dirk Bernhardt-Walther, Professor Sven Dickinson and Professor Michael Gruninger.

I obtained my Ph.D. and my Masters in Computer Science and miniMBA from McGill University where I was a Research Assistant in the Shape Analysis Group of Center for Intelligent Machines at McGill University working under the supervision of Professor Kaleem Siddiqi. I also received my bachelors in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology.
